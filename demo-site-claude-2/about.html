About Horizon Data Systems

Founded in 2012, Horizon Data Systems has become a leader in enterprise analytics and business intelligence. We help organizations transform raw data into actionable insights that drive growth.

Our Mission

To democratize data analytics, making powerful insights accessible to organizations of all sizes. We believe every business decision should be informed by data.

Our Story

Horizon was founded by Dr. Sarah Chen and Marcus Williams, two data scientists who saw a gap between enterprise data tools and what businesses actually needed. Starting in a small office in San Francisco, they built the first version of DataFlow in 2012.

By 2015, we had launched CloudVault, our cloud data warehouse platform. In 2019, we introduced InsightAI, bringing machine learning capabilities to our analytics suite.

Today, we serve over 2,000 enterprise customers across 40 countries, processing petabytes of data daily.

Our Values

  • Data-Driven - We practice what we preach, using data to guide our own decisions.
