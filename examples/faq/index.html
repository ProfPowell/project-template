Products

What's the difference between Widget Basic, Pro, and Enterprise? Widget Basic is our entry-level model, perfect for light-duty use. Widget Pro offers enhanced durability and a 5-year warranty - it's our best seller. Widget Enterprise is industrial-grade with aerospace-grade materials and a lifetime warranty. Are your products compatible with older models? Yes! All DemoCompany products maintain backward compatibility. Your new Widget Pro will work with accessories from products purchased years ago. Do you offer custom products? Absolutely. Our engineering team can design custom widgets, gadgets, and thingamajigs to meet your specific requirements. Contact our sales team at sales@democompany.com for custom quotes. What makes DemoCompany products different from competitors? Three things: quality, reliability, and support. We've been making widgets since 1952, and our products are built to last. Plus, our customer support team is available 24/7.

Ordering & Shipping

How long does shipping take? Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days within the US. Expedited shipping (2-3 days) and overnight options are available. International shipping varies by destination. Do you offer free shipping? Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $50 within the continental United States. Can I track my order? Yes. Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can also track orders by logging into your account. Do you ship internationally? Yes, we ship to over 50 countries. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout. Note that customers are responsible for any customs duties or taxes.

Returns & Warranty

What is your return policy? We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. Products must be unused and in original packaging. Contact customer support to initiate a return. How do I file a warranty claim? Contact our support team at support@democompany.com or call 1‑800‑555‑0199. Have your order number and product serial number ready. We'll guide you through the process. What does the warranty cover? Our warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship. It does not cover damage from misuse, accidents, or normal wear and tear. See our full warranty terms for details.

Account & Payment

What payment methods do you accept? We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay. Business customers can also pay by wire transfer or purchase order. Is my payment information secure? Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and are PCI-DSS compliant. We never store your full credit card number on our servers. How do I reset my password? Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. You'll receive a link to reset your password. If you don't see the email, check your spam folder.
