Senior Widget Engineer

Location: Upper Fake, WI
Department: Engineering
Type: Full-time
Experience: 5+ years
Salary: $95,000 - $130,000
Posted: December 1, 2024

About the Role

DemoCompany is seeking a Senior Widget Engineer to join our growing engineering team. In this role, you'll design and develop next-generation widgets that will shape the future of our product line. This is an exciting opportunity to work on cutting-edge technology while mentoring junior engineers.

Responsibilities

  • Design and develop new widget products from concept to production
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams including marketing, manufacturing, and quality assurance
  • Conduct testing and validation of widget prototypes
  • Create technical documentation and specifications
  • Mentor junior engineers and contribute to team development
  • Research new materials and manufacturing techniques
  • Support continuous improvement initiatives
  • Present technical findings to leadership

Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering or related field
  • 5+ years of experience in product development
  • Proficiency in CAD software (SolidWorks preferred)
  • Experience with manufacturing processes (injection molding, CNC machining)
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
  • Excellent communication and teamwork abilities
  • Experience with widget technology is a plus

Preferred Qualifications

  • Master's degree in Engineering
  • Professional Engineer (PE) license
  • Experience with Six Sigma or Lean manufacturing
  • Patent authorship
  • Industry certifications

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and annual bonus
  • Comprehensive health benefits
  • 401(k) with generous company match
  • Stock options
  • Professional development budget
  • Flexible work arrangements
  • Relocation assistance available
