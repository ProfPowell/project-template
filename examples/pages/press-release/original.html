DemoCompany Reports Record Q4 Earnings

Revenue Up 23% Year-Over-Year as Demand for Widgets Continues to Surge

UPPER FAKE, WISCONSIN - December 15, 2024 - DemoCompany (NASDAQ: DEMO), a leading manufacturer of widgets, gadgets, and thingamajigs, today announced financial results for the fourth quarter and full year ended December 31, 2024.

Q4 2024 Highlights

  • Revenue of $145 million, up 23% year-over-year
  • Net income of $28 million, up 31% year-over-year
  • Earnings per share of $1.42, exceeding analyst estimates of $1.28
  • Widget sales grew 27%, driven by strong demand for Widget Pro
  • Gadget segment revenue increased 18%
  • Thingamajig sales up 15% with continued market penetration

Full Year 2024 Results

  • Annual revenue of $500 million, a company record
  • Net income of $95 million
  • Launched 12 new products across all product lines
  • Expanded into 8 new international markets
  • Grew workforce by 15% to 2,500 employees

"2024 was a transformative year for DemoCompany," said George Winkleberry IV, Chief Executive Officer. "Our team's dedication to innovation and quality drove record results across all business segments. The successful launch of the Widget Pro series exceeded our expectations, and we're excited about the strong momentum heading into 2025."

Sarah Johnson, Chief Financial Officer, added: "Our financial performance reflects disciplined execution of our growth strategy. We achieved record revenue while expanding margins and generating strong free cash flow. We're well-positioned to continue investing in innovation while returning value to shareholders."

2025 Outlook

For fiscal year 2025, DemoCompany expects:

  • Revenue of $575-600 million, representing 15-20% growth
  • Continued margin expansion through operational efficiencies
  • Launch of next-generation gadget platform in Q2
  • Completion of European distribution center

About DemoCompany

DemoCompany is a leading manufacturer of widgets, gadgets, and thingamajigs headquartered in Upper Fake, Wisconsin. Founded in 1952, the company has grown from a family workshop to a publicly traded enterprise serving customers worldwide. DemoCompany is committed to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. For more information, visit www.democompany.com.
