What is a Thingamajig?

Great question! A thingamajig is our catch-all product category for specialized items that don't fit neatly into widgets or gadgets. They're custom-designed solutions for unique problems.

Installation Guide

Most thingamajigs require no installation - they're ready to use out of the box. For the Thingamajig Deluxe, follow these simple steps:

  1. Unbox your Thingamajig Deluxe
  2. Download the DemoCompany app from the App Store or Google Play
  3. Create an account or sign in
  4. Follow the in-app pairing instructions
  5. Customize your settings
  6. You're ready to go!
